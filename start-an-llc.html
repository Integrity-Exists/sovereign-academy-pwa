What’s an LLC (and why should you care)?

An LLC separates you from your business. If your business gets sued or goes into debt, your personal assets are protected.

  • ✔️ Easy to start
  • ✔️ Easy to manage
  • ✔️ Accepted by banks and the IRS
  • ✔️ You control it 100%

Step-by-Step: Start Your LLC in Virginia

  1. Pick Your Name: Search availability here.
  2. Registered Agent: Use your name or hire a service.
  3. File Articles of Organization: Online at Virginia SCC – $100.
  4. Get Your EIN: Free at irs.gov/ein.
  5. Write an Operating Agreement: We'll provide a template.
  6. Annual Report: Required in VA – $50/year.

Pro Tips from the Field

  • ✅ Keep personal and business money separate.
  • ✅ Use a PO Box if you want privacy.
  • ✅ Use smart naming: “Holdings,” “Group,” “Solutions,” etc.
