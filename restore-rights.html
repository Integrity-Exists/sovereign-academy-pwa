Restore Your Rights

Just because you were convicted doesn't mean you stay caged forever. Let’s talk about how to get your civil rights back — including the right to vote, own a firearm, and hold your head high again.

  • 🗳️ Voting Rights: In Virginia, apply for rights restoration here. This may include voting and serving on a jury.
  • 🔫 Firearm Rights: After civil rights are restored, you must petition the Circuit Court to restore firearm rights. It’s a second step.
  • 📜 Clemency or Pardon: You can also apply for a pardon through the Governor’s office for stronger protection and clearing your record.
  • 🧾 Expungement: Virginia’s expungement law is limited, but Sage can help you figure out if you qualify.
  • 🧠 Knowledge is Power: Use Sage to ask about eligibility, process, and filing strategies — customized by your conviction and status.
