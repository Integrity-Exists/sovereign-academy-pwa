Why Convert an LLC to a Trust?

  • 🛡️ Shields your business from lawsuits & divorce
  • 🏛️ Skips probate — pass it on instantly
  • 📜 Preserves control even after you're gone

What You Need First

  • ✔️ Active LLC (must be registered with the state)
  • ✔️ A revocable or irrevocable trust (notarized)
  • ✔️ Trust name, optional EIN, and trustee info

How to Transfer Your LLC to the Trust

  1. Draft a Trust Assignment of LLC Interest — declares trust as new owner
  2. Update your Operating Agreement — reflect the trust as member
  3. Notify your bank & IRS (optional) — update beneficial ownership
  4. Keep all records safe — notarized documents are your proof

Pros & Watch-Outs

  • ✅ Lawsuit protection & anonymity
  • ✅ Estate planning made easy
  • ⚠️ May affect business credit or loan eligibility
  • ⚠️ IRS may treat it as a taxable transfer — check with a CPA
