Landlord Rights

You’ve got property. You’ve got rules. And now you’ve got backup. Know your rights, keep it legal, and protect your investments without getting steamrolled.

  • 📄 Issue legal notices (nonpayment, lease violation, intent to vacate)
  • 🧾 Enforce lease agreements as written — don’t let them slide
  • 🛠 Access the unit legally with proper notice (typically 24–48 hrs)
  • ⚖️ Evict through court only — no self-help lockouts or retaliation
  • 🏚 Recover damages through security deposits and small claims
  • 📸 Document everything — pictures, communications, receipts
